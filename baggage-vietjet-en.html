Baggage is an important part of your flight journey. Therefore, to better meet the essential needs of each passenger, Vietjet Air will provide checked baggage and oversized luggage packages in accordance with Vietjet Air regulations (For more details, see the website).

Oversized baggage: A piece of baggage that exceeds the prescribed size of Vietjet Air.

- Checked Baggage size from 120cmx119cmx81cm to 200cmx119cmx81cm.

- Passenger can bring 1 oversized piece of baggage when pre-order on the Website and only applicable to flights operated by Vietjet (VJ).
