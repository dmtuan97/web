In order to serve passengers transporting apricot and peach blossoms on Lunar New Year 2021, Vietjet provides Tet flower delivery service via checked baggage (Details in website).

Passengers can transport 01 (one) bunch of apricot or peach in blossom.

Time application: The departure flight from January 10, 2021 to February 28, 2021 (ie from November 28 to January 17 on lunar calendar).

Conditions:

- Measuring maximum 150cm x 40cm x 40cm.

- Apricot and peach blossoms should be tied into bunches, sheathed and not more than 2 branches per bunch.

Notes:

- Vietjet does not transport the blossoms if they are in the form of the whole trees or still planted in pots, even by cargo

- Apply for domestic flights from and to Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Da Lat and Da Nang

- Flower shipping is depend on operating conditions of flight, maximum 30 punches/flight
