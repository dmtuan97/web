To fulfill the need of bring apricot and peach blossoms home during Lunar New Year 2022, Vietjet Air provides passengers with Tet flower delivery service:

Applied duration: Departure flights from January 12 to February 15, 2022 (or from December 10 to January 15 of Lunar calendar).

Conditions:

- Each passenger is allowed to carry 01 bouquet of flowers.

- Apricots and peach blossom are transported as checked baggage and should be tied into bouquet, carefully covered outside, and not exceed 02 branches/ bouquet.

- The size of each bouquet should not exceed 150cm x 40cm x 40cm.

Lưu ý:

- Apply for domestic flights flights from and to Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Da Lat and Da Nang.

- Vietjet Air does not transport the flowers in form of whole tree or planted in pots (even by cargo transportation).

- Each flight can carry no more than 30 bouquets.
